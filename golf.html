Eagle Sports Club Golf Academy

Eagle sports club Golf Academy is a FULL-TIME ELITE COMPETITIVE PLAYER PROGRAM. The Program is mentored by Rahul Ganapathy, who has attained the No. 1 rank in India at the Professional & Amateur Levels. The academy follows a holistic approach to teaching golf with a state-of-the-art fitting center which is equipped with a wide array of golf analysis and fitting tools that benefit all levels of golfers. Our teaching systems have been created by our senior coaches and club fitters as a result of their own learning and playing experience. With a combined playing experience of over 40 years, they have taken the best practices in golf and put together a system that helps golfers of all levels to achieve their best.

PROGRAM DETAILS

  • Duration: 6 days a week, 8 hours a day
  • Training Venue: Eagleton Golf Resort
  • Academic Venue: Eagle sports club
  • Other Facilities: Yoga training, Fitness Training, Mental health counseling, Nutrition Counseling, Medical checkups, Sports Physiotherapy, etc.

PROGRAM FEATURES

  • Weekly 1 on 1 Sessions with Mentors
  • 8hr Training Schedule Daily
  • Sport-specific Yoga and Fitness Sessions
  • Launch Monitor Use
  • Annual Club Fitting & Equipment Support On Services
  • Fitting Center And Studio Equipment Use

OUR TEAM

Rahul Ganapathy

Rahul has had an illustrious playing career during which he won multiple professional tournaments on tour. He also holds the Indian scoring record of 12 under (60). He has attained the No. 1 rank in India at the Professional and Amateur levels. He bagged the All India Amateur Championship in 1999 and went on to represent the nation in several international tournaments. He has been coaching for 13 years now, during which he has worked with several tour players, amateurs and juniors. He was the manager of the Hyderabad team during the Louis Phillippe Cup. As a coach, he has worked with over 1000 golfers.

Praduman Singh Guleria

Praduman is a former professional golfer who played on the Indian and Asian Tours. He represented the country in the Junior World Championships. Praduman is a proficient coach and club fitter with a TPI Level 2 and CAPTO Level 2 certification. With an experience of 10 years in club fitting, he has worked extensively on the equipment of over 20 top professional players on tour including Shubhankar Sharma and Ajeetesh Sandhu. He follows the principles of Proprioception and Positive Correlation to fit out players. He has been a coach to several professional and competition-level players in Delhi and Chandigarh.

Prannoy Kumar Shah Jagati

Prannoy has been teaching golf for over 5 years. Starting off as an apprentice to Praduman, he gained experience in the facets of Coaching and Club Fitting. Prannoy heads the Marketing and Promotions for Epic Golf. He also did a short stint at the Jaypee Greens Golf Academy as a Junior Coach and Club Builder. He has worked alongside Praduman on the equipment of several touring professionals. He has vast experience starting out beginners and conducting Junior Development Programs. Being a former footballer and gymnast, he works on fitness programs for juniors and sub juniors.
