EAGLE SPORTS CLUB BASKETBALL ACADEMY

The Sports School Basketball Academy with Key5 Coaching strives to empower students with knowledge and skills required to excel in the game while fostering positive attitude and competence. Key5 Coaching is the flagship coaching program by PGC Basketball who are the worldwide leader in teaching players how to think the game, be a leader, and run the show.
Our programs provide students with elite prospects to international level coaching, facilities, and competition along with a global framework that helps in maximizing their success. Our global standard basketball facilities allow students the best possible training experience.

GRL Prasad, Head Basketball Coach at TSS

GRL Prasad, Head Basketball Coach at TSS is an Army Professional and Sports Expert with vast Basketball Coaching Experience. He has coached the Indian Army Team & Senior National Basketball Team at Commonwealth Games, Asian Games, Asia Cup, South Asian Championship, Lusofinia Games, Asian Basketball Championship, Indian National Championship, etc. He has earned a Diploma in Sports Coaching from National Institute of Sports (NIS). He is a FIBA Level 1 and Level 2 Certified Coach along with B Panel National Referee. Additionally, he has attended various coaching clinics and seminars Internationally.
