CRICKET ACADEMY

The Sports Club Cricket Academy under the Mentorship of Robin Uthappa, provides aspiring cricketers with world-class training and best in class infrastructure to help them reach the next level. Our cricket program focuses on specialized coaching for batting, bowling, and wicket-keeping. The amount of training is assigned as per the skills and fitness level of the student, and adequate measures are taken care of to ensure the sport is fun for the beginners.

OVERIEW

  • The amount of training is assigned as per the skills and fitness level of the student
  • Fun-filled playing experience for Beginners
  • Regular focus on working on Technique (Basics)
  • Net sessions, match simulations, and matches
  • Technical and tactical know how
  • KSCA League Exposure

Specialized Training

  • Player Centered Coaching
  • Bowling machine sessions
